Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I book a taxi with Mishraji Tours?

You can book a taxi through our website using the booking form, by calling us at +91 74528 20984, or by visiting our office in Gayatri Nagar, Bareilly.

What types of vehicles do you offer?

We offer a wide range of vehicles including hatchbacks, sedans, SUVs, luxury cars, and Innovas to suit different travel needs and group sizes.

Do you provide outstation services?

Yes, we provide outstation services to all major cities and tourist destinations. We offer both one-way and round-trip options with experienced drivers.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup time without any charges. For cancellations made later, a nominal fee may apply.

Do you provide services for special occasions?

Yes, we provide decorated vehicles for weddings, parties, and other special events. We also offer professional chauffeurs for such occasions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI payments, and major credit/debit cards. Corporate clients can also avail monthly billing options.
